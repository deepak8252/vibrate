RESIDENTIAL INTERIOR

Your home reflects your personality. It's your own comfort space where you work and spend quality time with your family. Srishti Interiors carries the very essence of modern, contemporary functional yet having the personal touch and homely feeling of our clients. We specialize in designing upper-end custom homes for homeowners and builders. Our goal is to design homes that are aesthetically pleasing and functional according to each homeowner's lifestyle and needs.

The keystone of our design process lies in our attention to detail and dedication to excellence. Our home design packages include detailed floor plans featuring numerous elements that add a unique look to the home, which offers the home builder a refreshing alternative to today's typical suburban home. The features of residential interior design are that the designer focuses on single-family homes. This type of design includes several factors right from the minor features like Kitchen, Bath, Bedroom, Living, Dining, Outdoor, Kids Room, Home Office, Storage & Closets, Exterior, Basement, Entry Hall, flooring, lighting, and window decor to major things like furniture and wall paint and finish wall decor, decorative elements to enhance every corner, it covers everything.
