What Our Customers Have to Say

Akshay & Sudharma's Artistic Abode

Artists are made of their beliefs and inspirations. Sudharma is a classical dancer. Akshay is a mridangam artist. Their harmony and chemistry - in art and in life - is something to behold.

In working with Sudharma and Akshay, it was important that we understood how their minds worked. They saw life through the lens of art. Their space had to be designed in accordance with what they believed would be conducive to their respective art forms.

Take a tour through Sudharma and Akshay's artistic abode with “Symphony of Love.”

Shiv Nadar School

Nature holds the key to our aesthetic, intellectual, cognitive, and even spiritual satisfaction.

That's the belief upon which the world class sustainable school campus of Shiv Nadar School, Chennai is being developed. We are incredibly proud to be involved in this revolutionary project.

Conceptualized and designed by Padma Bhushan awardee Dr. B V Doshi, the Shiv Nadar School campus is a blend of modern aesthetic sensibilities with a keen focus on sustainability.

We are elated to be implementing the classrooms and other spaces of the campus with the singular objective of creating an atmosphere where students could thrive.

Agnikul IIT Madras

For AgniKul Cosmos, an aerospace manufacturer in the National Center for Combustion R&D of IIT Madras, Vibrant Spaces designed and executed their futuristic 13000+ square feet office. We are proud to have worked on this unique and revolutionary project.

Vibrant Spaces was chosen to design this futuristic marvel by the IIT Madras team out of many competing architectural/design firms. Designed with materials that reflect the work of Agnikul, we took an approach that represented the brand and their work authentically and effortlessly. This Star Trek based project was inaugurated by Shri. N. Chandrasekaran - Chairman TATA Sons, Shri. S. Somanath, Secretary -Department of Space & Chairman, ISRO and Prof V.Kamakoti - Director, IIT Madras.

Agnikul Cosmos was a one-of-a-kind project that required design chops that could tie the beauty of aerospace engineering to interior design. We also wanted to ensure that this could be a space that would benefit both their employees as well as their clients.
